OUR STORY

Bruteforce is an idea put together by
a group of enthusiasts, who believed
that students should have an exposure
to important technological instruments
and gadgets. We believe this should start
at the school level. With our experience
and exposure to the relevant technologies
of the present.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create a future
where the limits exist in human
imagination, not in human endeavor



We imagine a future in which the
journey from the birth of an idea
to it's fruition is optimised to be
simple; a future in which the ability
to create is not overshadowed
by the inability to access the
requisite tools and technologies.
A future in which boundaries are
defined by potential, not by practicality
