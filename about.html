DISRUPTING THE WAY
WE LEARN SCIENCE

Bruteforce is an idea put together by a group of enthusiasts, who believed that students should have an exposure to important technological instruments and gadgets. We believe this should start at the school level. With our experience and exposure to the relevant technologies of the present.

we want to share our expertise with the students by showcasing various technologies and their applications right in the respective school campuses. Students, as we know, boom with creativity in their younger years. Combined with our teachings and their creativity, we want to create an environment where students are allowed to know about the latest technologies and hopefully, create new ones in future.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create a future where the limits exist in human imagination, not in human endeavor



We imagine a future in which the journey from the birth of an idea to it's fruition is optimised to be simple; a future in which the ability to create is not overshadowed by the inability to access the requisite tools and technologies. A future in which boundaries are defined by potential, not by practicality
